About Dr. Nazifa Mubashir
Dr. Nazifa Mubashir is an obstetrician and gynecologist. After graduating from Dow Medical College she undertook postgraduate training in Obstetrics and Gynecology in Pakistan in a total year of 15 years. She also worked at centers in the UK. Her areas of expertise include: acute gynecology and early pregnancy, advanced antenatal practice, maternal medicine, subfertility and reproductive health, benign abdominal surgery (open and laparoscopic), benign gynecological surgery (Hysteroscopy) and advanced labour ward practice.
